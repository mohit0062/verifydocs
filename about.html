About VerifyDocs.in

Our Mission

In an era where digital privacy is increasingly under threat, VerifyDocs.in was built with a single core principle: Your data belongs to you.

Every day, millions of Indians share their Aadhaar, PAN, and other sensitive documents for various services. Often, people need to verify if a document number is correct before submitting it, or they need to mask their Aadhaar as per UIDAI guidelines. Most online tools require you to upload your files or enter your data into their servers.

"We believe you shouldn't have to sacrifice your privacy to use a simple validation tool."

VerifyDocs.in provides a suite of tools that run entirely in your browser. This means your document numbers and images never leave your computer or phone. No uploads, no databases, no tracking.

🛡️

Privacy First

We use client-side JavaScript to perform all validations. Your sensitive information is never sent to our servers. Even if our website is offline, the tools will still work if already loaded.

⚡

Built for Speed

No sign-ups, no ads, no pop-ups. Just clean, functional tools designed to give you instant results so you can get back to your work.

🇮🇳

Made in India

VerifyDocs.in is built by developers who understand the Indian digital ecosystem. We stay updated with the latest format changes from UIDAI, NSDL, and GSTN.

🆓

Free Forever

These essential tools will always be free to use. We believe basic security and validation utilities should be accessible to everyone.
