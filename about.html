Our Mission

Secure validation tools for Indian citizens, built with a "Privacy First" mindset.

Why verifydocs.online was built

In today's digital India, we frequently need to share document numbers like Aadhaar and PAN for KYC, bank applications, and government forms. Often, we find ourselves searching for "Aadhaar validator" or "PAN checker" only to find websites that ask us to upload images or submit numbers to their servers.

As a creator from India, I was concerned about how much sensitive data is being collected by third-party tools. verifydocs.online was built to fix this. My goal was to create a set of tools that are powerful enough to help you catch errors, but simple enough that they never need to "see" your data.

The "Zero-Upload" Promise

The core philosophy of verifydocs.online is simple: Your data should never leave your device. Every single tool on this website—from the Aadhaar Verhoeff algorithm to the PAN decoder—runs entirely using JavaScript in your own web browser. No databases, no storage, no logs. Just instant, private validation.

Who is behind this?

verifydocs.online is an independent project maintained by a developer passionate about utility tools and digital privacy in India. I believe that citizens deserve free, high-quality tools that don't treat their personal data as a product.

I am committed to keeping these tools free forever, supported only by minimal, non-intrusive advertising to cover hosting costs. My focus remains on adding more validators and providing educational content through our blog to help users understand the structure and security of their documents.
