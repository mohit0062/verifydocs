GSTIN Full Form & Structure

GSTIN stands for Goods and Services Tax Identification Number. It is a 15-character alphanumeric code assigned to every GST-registered business in India. Example: 27AABCU9603R1ZX

PositionExampleWhat It Means
1–227State Code (Maharashtra = 27)
3–12AABCU9603RPAN of the registered entity
131Entity number (1st registration in state)
14ZAlways 'Z' (default)
15XCheck digit (letter or number)

Step 2: PAN Decode Karo (Characters 3–12)

Characters 3 to 12 wahi PAN hota hai jo business ne Income Tax Department se liya hua hai. Yahan se entity type bhi pata chalta hai:

  • Agar 4th character of PAN (i.e., 6th character of GSTIN) 'P' hai → Individual person
  • 'C' hai → Company/Private Ltd
  • 'F' hai → Partnership firm
  • 'H' hai → HUF

Step 3: Company Ka Actual Naam Kaise Pata Kare

GSTIN se directly company ka naam format mein encode nahi hota. Naam ke liye 2 official options hain:

  1. GST Portal: gst.gov.in/searchtaxpayer → "Search Taxpayer" → Enter GSTIN → Company name, registration date, status sab milega.
  2. Invoice Verification: GST-registered sellers are legally required to print their registered business name on invoices. If the name on invoice doesn't match the GST portal — it could be fraud.

Fake GSTIN Se Kaise Bachein

Common fraud patterns in Indian business:

  • GSTIN ke 14th character mein 'Z' ke alawa kuch aur hona — invalid
  • GSTIN sirf 14 ya 16 characters ka hona — invalid
  • State code aur company address ka mismatch — suspicious
  • GSTIN ka GST portal pe exist na karna — definitely fake

Character 13: Entity Number

This digit (1–9 or A–Z) tells you how many GST registrations the business has in that state. Most businesses have '1'. If you see '2' or '3', the company has multiple business verticals registered separately in the same state.

Character 15: Check Digit

The last character is a check digit computed using a MOD-36 algorithm (similar to Luhn's algorithm). If this fails validation, the entire GSTIN is mathematically invalid — regardless of how legitimate it looks.

Frequently Asked Questions

Ek company ke kitne GSTIN ho sakte hain?

Jitne states mein business operate karta hai utne. Par ek hi state mein ek se zyada verticals ke liye alag GSTIN milta hai (13th character 1, 2, 3... badalta hai).

Kya GSTIN cancel ho sakta hai?

Haan. GST officer ya taxpayer khud cancellation request kar sakta hai. Cancelled GSTIN wale se invoice lena risky hai — GST portal pe status check karo.

Freelancers ko GSTIN ki zaroorat hai?

Agar annual turnover ₹20 lakh se kam hai (services) to GST registration mandatory nahi. Threshold states ke hisaab se vary karta hai.
