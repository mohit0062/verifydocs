How to Check if Aadhaar is Linked to Bank Account Online

Linking your Aadhaar card to your bank account is essential for receiving government benefits, subsidies, and using Aadhaar-enabled payment services. If you're unsure whether your linking was successful, you can check the status online in just a few minutes.

In this guide, we will explore the three official ways to check your Aadhaar-bank account linking status.

Method 1: Using the Official UIDAI (MyAadhaar) Portal

The most reliable way to check your linking status is through the UIDAI website. Note that this service reflects data from the NPCI (National Payments Corporation of India) mapper.

  1. Visit the official portal: myaadhaar.uidai.gov.in
  2. Click on "Login" and enter your 12-digit Aadhaar number and the Captcha code.
  3. Authenticate with the OTP sent to your Aadhaar-registered mobile number.
  4. Once logged in, look for the "Bank Seeding Status" service.
  5. The screen will display your Aadhaar number, bank name, and the status (Active/Inactive) of the linking.

Method 2: Using the NPCI Bharat Aadhaar Seeding Enabler (BASE)

NPCI provides a dedicated portal for checking Aadhaar seeding status across banks.

  1. Go to the NPCI Check Aadhaar Status page.
  2. Enter your 12-digit Aadhaar number.
  3. Complete the security verification.
  4. Check the status shown on the result page.

Method 3: Using USSD Code (On Mobile)

If you don't have internet access, you can check the status using any mobile phone (it doesn't have to be a smartphone).

  1. Dial *99*99*1# from your Aadhaar-registered mobile number.
  2. Enter your 12-digit Aadhaar number.
  3. Confirm the number when prompted.
  4. The screen will show the bank account that was last linked to your Aadhaar.

What Does "Active" Status Mean?

An "Active" status means that your Aadhaar number is currently mapped to a specific bank account in the NPCI database. This account will be used to receive all your DBT (Direct Benefit Transfer) payments from the government.

What to Do if Status is "Inactive" or Incorrect?

If the status is inactive or shows a bank account you no longer use:

  • Visit your local bank branch with your Aadhaar card.
  • Fill out a "Mandate for Aadhaar Seeding" form.
  • The bank will update your details in the NPCI mapper, which usually takes 2-4 working days.

Important Privacy Tip

When using third-party verification tools or checking status online, always ensure you are on an official .gov.in or .org.in website. Never share your Aadhaar OTP with anyone calling you on the phone claiming to be from a bank.

Frequently Asked Questions

Does linking Aadhaar automatically enable DBT?

No, you need to provide a separate "Consent for DBT" to your bank to start receiving government benefits in that specific account.

Can I link two bank accounts to the same Aadhaar?

While you can link multiple accounts to your Aadhaar, only one account can be "seeded" for receiving government benefits through NPCI at any given time.
