How to Validate Your Aadhaar Number Online in India

Aadhaar is one of the most important identity documents in India. It is a 12-digit unique identification number issued by UIDAI. Many people search online to check whether an Aadhaar number is valid in format or not.

What is Aadhaar Validation?

Aadhaar validation means checking whether the entered Aadhaar number follows the correct 12-digit format and checksum algorithm called the Verhoeff Algorithm.

What is Verhoeff Algorithm?

The Verhoeff algorithm is a checksum formula used to detect errors in Aadhaar numbers.

Why Aadhaar Validation is Important?

It helps prevent mistakes while filling forms, KYC verification, and identity-related processes.
