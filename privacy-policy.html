Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 26, 2024

At VerifyDocs.in, we take your privacy extremely seriously. Unlike many other validation tools, we have designed our platform to ensure that your sensitive document data never leaves your device.

1. The "Browser-Only" Guarantee

Our core technical principle is client-side processing. This means:

  • When you enter an Aadhaar, PAN, or GST number, the validation logic runs entirely in your web browser (using JavaScript).
  • Your document numbers are never uploaded to our servers.
  • Your data is not stored in any database.
  • We do not have access to the information you type into the validation boxes.

2. Data We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect or store:

  • Aadhaar numbers (UIDs)
  • PAN card details
  • GST identification numbers
  • IFSC codes or bank details
  • Voter ID / Passport / Driving License numbers
  • Images or PDFs uploaded for masking (processed in-memory in your browser)

3. Information We Collect

To improve our service and understand general usage patterns, we may collect minimal non-personally identifiable information:

  • Analytics: We use lightweight analytics to track page views and which tools are popular. This does not include any data you enter into the tools.
  • Cookies: We may use basic cookies to remember your preferences (like dark mode, if applicable).
  • Server Logs: Standard web server logs collect basic information like IP addresses and browser types for security and maintenance purposes, but this is never linked to document validation data.

4. Aadhaar Masking Tool

Our Aadhaar Masking tool uses your browser's processing power to modify images/PDFs. The files you select are loaded locally and are never transmitted over the internet. Once you close the tab, the data is gone from your browser's memory.

5. External Links

Our website may contain links to government portals (like UIDAI or GSTN) for your convenience. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these external sites.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at privacy@verifydocs.in.
