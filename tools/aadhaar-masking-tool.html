Why Should You Mask Your Aadhaar?

🛡️

UIDAI Recommendation

UIDAI officially recommends using Masked Aadhaar for routine KYC submissions. Sharing a full Aadhaar is not required for most purposes.

🔒

Prevent Identity Misuse

Your full 12-digit Aadhaar linked to biometrics can potentially be misused. Masking limits exposure while still confirming your identity.

✅

Widely Accepted

Masked Aadhaar (showing last 4 digits) is accepted by most private entities for KYC — banks, landlords, telecom operators.

📋

Digital Personal Data Protection

Under India's DPDP Act 2023, minimizing data shared is a good practice. Masked Aadhaar is a simple way to share less.
