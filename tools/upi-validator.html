UPI ID Validator — Check VPA Format Online (Free)

Last updated: 2026-05-02

Unified Payments Interface (UPI) has revolutionized payments in India. Every UPI user has a unique Virtual Payment Address (VPA), commonly known as a UPI ID. Our UPI ID Validator helps you verify if a UPI ID follows the correct username@bank structure.

Using an incorrect UPI ID can lead to failed payments or money being sent to the wrong person. While most UPI apps verify the ID before payment, our tool is useful for developers and businesses who need to validate the structure of UPI IDs during data entry. 100% browser-side and private.

What is a UPI ID (VPA)?

A UPI ID is an identifier unique to a person for sending and receiving money. It works like an email address for your bank account. The structure is typically:

  • Handle/Username: Can contain letters, numbers, and dots (e.g., 9876543210, name.surname).
  • Separator: The '@' symbol.
  • PSP (Payment Service Provider) Code: Represents the bank or app (e.g., okhdfcbank, ybl, paytm).
