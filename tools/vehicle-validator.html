Vehicle Number Validator

Check if an Indian vehicle registration number (Number Plate) is in the correct format. This tool identifies the state of registration and validates the RTO structure.

Example: DL 01 C 1234, MH 12 AB 5678, KA 03 9999

Registration Number Breakdown

1234

1. State Code: The first two letters indicate the State or Union Territory where the vehicle is registered (e.g., MH for Maharashtra, DL for Delhi).

2. District Code: The next two digits indicate the specific RTO (Regional Transport Office) within that state.

3. Series: One, two, or three letters that indicate the registration series. Some older vehicles or specific types might not have this.

4. Unique Number: A 4-digit number unique to each vehicle in that series.
