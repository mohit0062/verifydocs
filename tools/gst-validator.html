GST Number Validator (GSTIN Checker)

Verify if a GSTIN (GST Identification Number) is in the correct 15-character format as assigned by the GST Council. Decode the state code, extract the embedded PAN, entity number, and check digit — all instantly in your browser.

15 alphanumeric characters. Example: 27AABCU9603R1ZX

Note: Format validation only. Does not verify registration status on GST Portal (gst.gov.in).

Frequently Asked Questions

GSTIN stands for Goods and Services Tax Identification Number. It is a unique 15-digit alphanumeric identifier assigned to every GST-registered business in India. It encodes the state, PAN, and entity registration number of the business.
Yes. A business operating in multiple states must register in each state separately and gets a unique GSTIN per state. The state code (first 2 digits) differs for each, but the embedded PAN (characters 3–12) remains the same. The entity number (character 13) distinguishes multiple registrations within the same state.
The 14th character 'Z' is a reserved constant defined by the GST Council when the GSTIN format was designed. It acts as a separator before the final check digit. It's always Z for all regular taxpayers in India.
