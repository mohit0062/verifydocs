Indian Passport Validator

Indian passport numbers are exactly 8 characters long, starting with a letter followed by 7 digits. Our tool helps you verify the format and identifies the type of passport (e.g., P for Personal/Regular).

Passport Types in India

  • P (Regular): Personal passport issued to ordinary citizens.
  • S (Service): Official passport issued to government officials.
  • D (Diplomatic): Issued to top-ranking government officials and diplomats.
