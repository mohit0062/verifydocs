About Voter ID (EPIC)

The Electors Photo Identity Card (EPIC) is a government-issued photo identity card issued by the Election Commission of India. It serves both as proof of identity and as the voter's card for casting votes in Indian elections.

The EPIC number uniquely identifies each voter on India's electoral roll. It is a 10-character alphanumeric code: the first 3 letters encode the constituency, and the last 7 digits are a sequential serial number.
